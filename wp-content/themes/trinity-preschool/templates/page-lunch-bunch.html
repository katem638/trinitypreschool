Extra Care · Lunch Bunch

Pack a lunch. We've got the rest.

A teacher-led hour after morning class - for lunch, free play, and a little more time with friends - every day until 1:30 PM.

The Program

The Hour

From noon-ish to 1:30 PM

When morning class wraps, Lunch Bunch friends head to the Lunch Bunch room. Same teachers your child already knows, same building, no transitions to navigate.

The Lunch

Bring your own - no peanut butter.

Pack a lunch from home in a labeled bag or box. We'll keep it cold in our refrigerator and pull it out when it's time to sit down and eat together.

After Lunch

Free play, or outside if the weather cooperates.

Once everyone's eaten, the rest of the hour is open - favorite classroom corners indoors, or the playground when it's nice out.
