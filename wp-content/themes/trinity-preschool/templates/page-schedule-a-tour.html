Common questions

Do I bring my child to the tour?

Yes. Tours are best when your child can peek into rooms, see teachers in motion, and get a feel for the school day.

What if we can't make a weekday morning?

Tell us what is possible in the form. Weekday mornings are ideal, but we will help you find a time that works.

Can we see the playground?

Absolutely. Weather permitting, we will include the playground and outdoor spaces on your visit.

Do you have a waitlist for fall?

Availability changes by age group. Send your child's school year and we will reply with the current status.
